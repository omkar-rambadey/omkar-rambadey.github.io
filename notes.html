The Ewald Sphere Construction

In reciprocal space, the condition for diffraction is given by the vector relation:

\[ \mathbf{q} = \mathbf{k}_o - \mathbf{k}_i = \mathbf{G} \]

Where \( \mathbf{k}_i \) is the incident wave vector and \( \mathbf{G} \) is a reciprocal lattice vector. The center of the Ewald sphere is located at the tail of \( \mathbf{k}_i \).
